Initial (v1.0)
1. Computer will randomly pick one: rock, paper or scissors
2. Ask user to pick one: rock, paper or scissors
3. Decide the winner based on the choices
4. Display a message with the outcome
5. Record the outcome and keep track of the score
6. Play again (for a pre-set number of rounds)

Revised (v2.0)
1. User will click a button to make their selection
2. Computer will make a random selection
3. Decide the winner based on the choices
4. Animation to play after clicking
5. Results to be displayed as text
6. Record the outcome and keep track of the score
7. Play again (for a pre-set number of rounds)
